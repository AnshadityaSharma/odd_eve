How does the AI learn?

The AI adapts to YOU in real-time using a hybrid prediction model:

Markov Chain (50%) Tracks what you throw AFTER each move. If you often throw 3 after a 5, it learns that transition.
Frequency Analysis (30%) Counts how often you use each number overall. If you favor 2 and 4, it notices.
Recency Bias (20%) Weighs your last 8 throws more heavily, detecting when you start repeating patterns.

When bowling, the AI picks your most likely throw (to get you OUT). When batting, it avoids your predicted throw. Learning persists across sessions.
